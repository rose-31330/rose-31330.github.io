Welcome to my personal website. I'm Rose — an aerospace engineering student at Queen Mary University of London with a particular interest in spacecraft systems engineering and remote sensing.

I built this site as a space to share my projects, write about things I'm learning, and have somewhere to point people when they ask what I've been up to.

## What to expect here

I'll be writing about:

- **Projects** — the rocketry competition, the QMSAT CubeSat, and whatever comes next
- **Things I'm learning** — from spacecraft dynamics and mission analysis to Python data pipelines and systems engineering methods
- **Industry experiences** — reflections from my internship at the Satellite Applications Catapult and beyond

## A bit about me

I'm French-British, studying an integrated MEng at QMUL. Outside of engineering, I'm President of the Mile End Music Society — leading six ensembles and about 250 members. Music and engineering keep me busy, which is exactly how I like it.
